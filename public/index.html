About Us

Thingaha donator group is currently based in Tokyo, Japan. In Myanmar, we have connected with the president of monastic schools in Ayeyarwaddy division who actually handle actual payment, accounting and everything else on the ground.

President of Monastic Schools Adminstration Council, Nyaung Done Township.

U Yaewatta oversees and manages staffing, finances and administration of 5 monastic schools within Nyaung Done township. He helps Thingaha donators connect with those children and handle the actual payment logistics in Myanmar side.
