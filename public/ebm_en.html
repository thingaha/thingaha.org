Education-based Mentorship Programme

We are currently trying to give mental support and guidance to the youths of Myanmar kids who lost formal education because of unstable conditions in Myanmar right now. In a group, we connect one student per one educational guide with the technical help of one volunteer from Thingaha. Our main target is to show one learning accomplishment of the students after each 12-month program.

Mentors/Guide/Donator

As a Mentor, minimum 5 hours contribution is needed per month throughout one year. Mentor has responsibilities to provide guidance, advice, feedback and support to the participant. Mentor have to report participant progress to Thingaha Volunteer Group and make discussion if it is needed.

Participants/Students

This is one year programme to offer the students under age of 20 who join in the CDM. Participants must have passion and are willing to join all year long.

Volunteers (Thingaha)

Volunteers are medium between Students and Guides to check progress and take record for monthly meeting. At Least 8 hours contributions is needed per month throughout one year.

Pilot Project Round 1 - 2023

Thingaha Volunteers 15 Persons, Mentors/Guides/Donators 15 Persons, Participants 15 Persons, and Duration One Year

Project Timeline

  • PERIOD 1 JAN - MAR

    Meeting with mentor and participant

    • During first 3 months Mentor and Participant try to be familiar each other
    • Participant's interests and needs should be discussed after building open friendship
  • PERIOD 2 APR - JUN

    Define a topic to be supported

    • Depend on the participant's needs, the mentor will decide a course of study
    • Mentor will implement to support financially or intellectually
  • PERIOD 3 JUL - SEP

    Learning Period

    • This period will start once Mentor, Participants and Volunteers decide the supporting way
    • Whether supporting by a mentor or joining the class, Mentor have to check their progress and review what they have learnt. Mentor need to offer special meeting or supports if it is needed.
  • PERIOD 4 OCT - DEC

    Reviewing Period

    • Whether participants are still learning or have already done, Mentor have to discuss with participants what the knowledge gained and that can be applied in real life.
    • If there is any requirements, it will be reported to the Thingaha Group to get further discussion.
  • Finish
Conclusion

The purpose of this project is to help the students in the CDM who lost the way in the current situation to find their hobbies and new things they have passion to learn.

During this situation, we intend to help the students who need financial assists, mental supports and their needs for their growth. The ultimate goal of Thingaha is what they have learnt in programme are able to apply and useful in real life.

The action of the upcoming plan in 2023 is actually needed for critical support and Thingaha is also hereby to provide in an actual help to those students.

We believe that our ability of guidance is certainly making contact with the students who need mental and physical support under the current situation in Myanmar.
